# Data Access Notes

This project uses the UK Labour Force Survey Two-Quarter Longitudinal Datasets for 2013–2015 and 2017–2018, accessed through the UK Data Service.

The raw data is not included in this repository because access may be subject to licensing restrictions.

To reproduce the analysis, users should obtain the relevant Labour Force Survey longitudinal datasets through the UK Data Service and run the Stata do-file contained in the `code/` folder.

The cleaned dataset is also not included publicly unless permitted by the relevant data licence.
